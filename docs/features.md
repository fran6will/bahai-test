# Features & Pages

## Pages Overview

### 1. Home Page (`/`)
The landing page serves as an introduction to the community and its activities.
- **Hero Section**: Welcomes visitors with a large background image and introductory text.
- **Community Section**: Highlights the community's focus on friendship and service.
- **Activities**: A list of core activities (Community spaces, Study circles, Children/Pre-youth, Social action) presented with images and descriptions.
- **Unity in Diversity**: Explains the core Bahá'í principle of unity.
- **Sanctuary Preview**: A teaser for the Sanctuary page with a call to action.

### 2. Vision (`/vision`)
Explains the beliefs and principles of the Bahá'í Faith.
- **Beliefs**: Discusses the concept of God and the unity of religion.
- **Principles**: detailed cards explaining key principles such as:
    - Independent investigation of truth
    - Harmony between science and religion
    - Gender equality
    - Universal education
    - Economic justice

### 3. Sanctuary (`/sanctuary`)
Dedicated to the Bahá'í Shrine of Montreal (the Maxwell home).
- **History**: Describes the significance of the house and 'Abdu'l-Bahá's visit in 1912.
- **Visiting**: Information about visiting hours and tours.
- **Booking**: Integrated Calendly iframe for scheduling visits.

### 4. Contact (`/contact`)
Allows visitors to get in touch.
- **Contact Form**: A form to send messages with subject selection (General, Activities, Visit, etc.).
- **Contact Info**: Addresses, emails, and phone numbers for the Bahá'í Center, Bookstore, and Sanctuary.
- **Opening Hours**: Lists opening hours for the Bookstore and Sanctuary.

### 5. Other Pages
- **Fields of Action (`/champs-action`)**: (Implied) Detailed view of community activities.
- **News (`/news`)**: (Implied) Updates and news articles.
- **FAQ (`/faq`)**: (Implied) Frequently asked questions.

## User Flows

### Discovering the Community
1.  User lands on **Home**.
2.  Reads about the community and activities.
3.  Clicks "Learn more about activities" to go to **Fields of Action**.

### Learning about Beliefs
1.  User navigates to **Vision** via the menu or the "Unity in Diversity" section on Home.
2.  Reads about the core beliefs and principles.

### Booking a Sanctuary Visit
1.  User navigates to **Sanctuary**.
2.  Learns about the history.
3.  Uses the embedded **Calendly** widget to book a time slot.

### Contacting the Community
1.  User navigates to **Contact**.
2.  Fills out the form with a specific subject.
3.  Submits the form (handled by backend/API).
